About

I’m Sabbir Muhammad Saleh from Dhaka, currently studying Computer Science as a Ph.D. student at the University of Western Ontario, Canada. Previously I worked as an Assistant Professor (currently on Study Leave) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at City University, Dhaka. I have also worked as a Lecturer & Department Coordinator of the Computer Science & Engineering Department at the University of South Asia. I have my BSc in Computer Science and MSc in Computer Science (Software Engineering) from American International University-Bangladesh.
